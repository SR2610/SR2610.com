Hi I'm Stephen!

I'm a games developer currently working at NSC Creative on immersive VR Experiences.  I graduated from Solent University in 2019 with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Games (Indie).
In my free time I can be found working on either game prototypes, mods, or geeking out over space.

On this website you can find both games and mods that I have developed.  The primary tools that I use for creating games is Unity and C#.
